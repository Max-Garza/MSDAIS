We can determine L and Lq by:

$$L = \lambda W$$
$$L = L_q + \frac{\lambda}{\mu}$$
$$L_q = \lambda W_q$$L = total customers in the system
lambda = arrival rate
W = total expected waiting time
Lq = customers in the queue
Wq = expected queueing time
mu = mean service rate

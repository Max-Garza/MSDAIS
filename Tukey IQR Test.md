#111ANLY 
Method of outlier identification that states *x* is an outlier if:

$$x >= q_3 + 1.5(q_3-q_1)$$
$$x <= q_1 - 1.5(q_3-q_1)$$
Where:
- *x* = datum in a variable
- *q3* = 75th percentile of a variable
- *q1* = 25th percentile of a variable

Appropriate for non-multi-modal variables
